Cancer Research

Broadly speaking, my primary research interests revolve around various aspects of cancer research. I'm interested in how our current drug molecules (for any brain disorder) pass through the blood-brain barrier, along with the rate at which they do so. This barrier is a common issue as certain drug molecules can be effective at treating a particular disease/disorder, yet very ineffectively cross the blood-brain barrier. Using this knowledge i believe better drug screening practices could be implemented so better treatments for all brain diseases (including brain cancer) could arise, and at a quicker pace by ruling out molecules that are less likely to be effective at both treatment and navigation to the molecule's target.
